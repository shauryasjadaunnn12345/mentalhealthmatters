What is Schizophrenia?

Schizophrenia is a chronic brain disorder that affects how a person thinks, feels, and behaves. Symptoms may include delusions, hallucinations, trouble with thinking and concentration, and lack of motivation. Early screening and diagnosis can help in better outcomes through proper support and treatment.
