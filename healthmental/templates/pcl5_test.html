What is PCL-5?

The PCL-5 (Posttraumatic Stress Disorder Checklist for DSM-5) is a self-report measure used to assess symptoms of PTSD in individuals. It consists of 20 questions related to the DSM-5 criteria for PTSD.

Instructions

Please respond to each question based on how often you have been bothered by the symptoms in the past month. Choose the response that best represents your experience for each item.
