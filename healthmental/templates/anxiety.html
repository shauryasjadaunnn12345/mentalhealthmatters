Symptoms of Anxiety & Depression

Persistent Worry

Constant feelings of worry, panic, or fear that interfere with daily activities

Social Withdrawal

Avoiding social interactions or situations due to fear or anxiety

Fatigue & Sleep Issues

Feeling tired, having trouble sleeping, or experiencing muscle tension

Hopelessness

Persistent feelings of sadness, emptiness, or hopelessness about the future
